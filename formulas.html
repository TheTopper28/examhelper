💰 Comparing Quantities (Tax & Discount)

Discount = Marked Price × Discount% / 100
Selling Price (after discount) = Marked Price − Discount
Selling Price = Marked Price × (100 − Discount%) / 100
GST Amount = Price × GST% / 100
Price including GST = Price + GST
Price including GST = Price × (100 + GST%) / 100
Final Price (Discount then GST) = (Marked Price − Discount) + GST
Final Price = Marked Price × (100 − Discount%) / 100 × (100 + GST%) / 100

📦 Cylinder

➗ Algebraic Identities
